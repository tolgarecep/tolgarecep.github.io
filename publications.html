  1. "Locating a Nearest Rectangular Polynomial Matrix with a Prescribed Eigenvalue.", is a joint work with Professor Emre Mengi, Professor Paul Van Dooren, Assoc. Prof. Vanni Noferini and Dr. Lauri Nyman. Given a rectangular matrix polynomial, we estimate the distance to a nearest rectangular polynomial with an eigenvalue under general complex perturbations, as well as more restricted set of real perturbations. The code is published at https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.17077517. The paper will soon be submitted for publication and made available on math arXiv.
